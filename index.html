Why choose AAP?

In AAP, we care for the well-being of athletes in Singapore as we believe that athletes deserve the peace of mind that they feel assured and protected in an event of an accident. We also believe in supporting Sports Organizations and school sports teams to bring out their full potential from assisting in possible injuries during competitions, to accidental deaths.

Our Coverages

  • Property: Damage to facilities, equipments, and events.
  • Personal Accidents: Medical expenses, hospital cash benefits, emergency ambulance fees, permanant or partial disability, and accidental death.
